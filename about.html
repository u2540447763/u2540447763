About

GreenLeaf Yoga began as a pop‑up in a tiny plant shop. Today, we’re a sunny community studio focused on accessibility, curiosity, and joy.

Our mission

To make yoga welcoming and transformative for everyone, regardless of background, ability, or experience.

Values

  • Kindness over competitiveness
  • Progress over perfection
  • Community over ego

GreenLeaf Yoga

Mindful movement for every body. Join our inclusive community and grow on and off the mat.
